Accessibility Statement
Our Commitment to Accessibility

We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone, and applying the relevant accessibility standards to help users with various disabilities access our website effectively.

Compliance Status

Our website strives to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. We also aim to be compliant with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and the European Accessibility Act requirements. These guidelines and regulations explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities. We acknowledge that some aspects of our website may not yet achieve full compliance, and we are actively working to address these areas.

Accessibility Features Available

Our website implements the Accessibly App, which provides a variety of tools to enhance website accessibility:

Visual Adjustments
  • Bigger Text: Increase text size up to 3x for better readability (WCAG 2.1/1.4.4)
  • Bigger Cursor: Enhanced cursor visibility for easier navigation
  • Color Adjustments: Invert colors, adjust contrast, brightness, and saturation
  • Grayscale Mode: Convert website to grayscale for users with visual impairments
  • Hide Images: Reduce visual distractions for easier reading
Reading and Navigation Aids
  • Reading Line: Adds a guide line to help follow text
  • Reading Mask: Places a mask over text to isolate lines and enhance focus
  • Readable Fonts: Converts to highly legible fonts for better comprehension
  • Highlight Links: Makes links more prominent for easier navigation
  • Dyslexic Fonts: Special typography for users with dyslexia
  • Page Structure: Simplified layout options for easier navigation
Assistive Technologies
  • Keyboard Navigation: Full website control using keyboard (Tab, Shift+Tab, Enter)
  • Alt Text for Images: AI-generated image descriptions where manual ones aren't provided
  • Read Page Aloud: Text-to-speech functionality
  • Stop Animations: Pause motion content for users with vestibular disorders
Limitations and Feedback

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility, there may be some limitations. Content provided by third parties, user-generated content, or certain legacy pages may not be fully accessible. We are continuously working to improve our website's accessibility.

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us. We are committed to addressing these issues promptly.

Technical Information

The accessibility features on this website are provided through the Accessibly App, which utilizes several technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks to enhance accessibility. Our implementation strives to be compatible with major screen readers and assistive technologies.

How to Decorate a Bathroom

The question of how to decorate a bathroom introduces a balance of consideration for form and function. From the choice of materials and fixtures to storage and personal details, every decision regarding bathroom decor plays a role in shaping this intimate space.


The bathroom is an escape for the most personal rituals and daily self care, where functional bathroom decoration ideas allow this intimate room to exist as a holistic interior within any space.

SHOP BATHROOM ESSENTIALS
Where to start?


1. Find the right mirror for your bathroom


In their capacity to open up a small space, bathroom wall mirrors are more than just a functional item. What kind of mirrors are best for bathrooms? Whether it be small or large bathroom mirrors, a mirror will bring in timeless simplicity with modern utility.


Rectangular mirrors, in their confident shape, add structure and order to an otherwise cluttered space. The straight lines and angles of square bathroom mirrors can help create a sense of symmetry and balance in the room. Rectangular, framed bathroom mirrors are also more practical in some environments with space constraints. The FRAMA Rectangular Mirror is an elevated version of a classic mirror. Its refined lines and understated style make it an excellent choice for any bathroom, bringing elegance and simplicity into a practical space.


Round bathroom mirrors also have a variety of benefits. One of the most noticeable is that they can add softness to an otherwise angular space. Round mirrors for the bathroom can help to create a more balanced and visually appealing atmosphere, especially if your bathroom already has many straight lines and sharp corners. Round mirrors can also help to add depth and dimension to a room. They can make your bathroom feel larger and more open without any corners or edges to cut off the space. They have a classic, timeless quality that complements any bathroom. The Circular Mirror Collection is an example of how the purity of the circular form can be utilized to create a timeless design.

2. Make room for self care in your bathroom


When it comes to creating a relaxing bathroom experience with bathroom decor, it's important to not only focus on functionality but also on atmosphere. After all, the bathroom is a space where you can escape from the outside world. When designing your bathroom, consider how you can incorporate self care products and home fragrances as decorative bathroom accessories into the space.

Elevate your bathroom by showcasing your preferred soap or body wash in a sophisticated soap dish or bottle. To create an even more elegant look, you can also choose one of our wall mount soap dispensers, designed specifically to showcase our St. Pauls Apothecary collection products. These stylish displays provide a chic and visually appealing way to display self care products in your bathroom; particularly products that will be in frequent use like hand care product essentials.

By adding these small touches, you can create a relaxing and inviting atmosphere in your bathroom. Let our collection of self care products inspire you to transform your bathroom into a haven of relaxation and calmness. Our selection is thoughtfully chosen to not only nourish your body and mind, but also refine the look of your bathroom — and should be applied seated at the right bathroom stool or bathroom chair, too. And how do you pick a good bath towel? In both heavy and light weights, FRAMA towels are produced with complementary use with our care products in mind.

3. Find storage solutions for your bathroom


Bathroom storage is more than just a necessity, it can bring serenity to the most private spaces.


Storage is an integral part of any well-designed and organized bathroom. Bathroom storage can be both functional and visually appealing, bringing a simple, straightforward elegance to the room. Discover storage that can declutter and exist as an object of display in and of itself. 


Bathroom hanging cabinets are an excellent option for storing larger items like bathroom towels, hand towels for the bathroom, self care products, and toiletries. Bathroom wall display cabinets are especially useful for freeing up floor space and creating room to move in the bathroom. How to store things in a small bathroom? Consider investing in a narrow cabinet as a bathroom storage idea for small spaces — minimalist design with a generous, practical purpose.

4. Shelves and boxes as bathroom organization ideas


FRAMA bathroom storage solutions are designed to bring versatility and functionality to an essential interior, particularly with the inclusion of bathroom wall-mounted bathroom shelves.

Shelves play a pivotal role in small bathrooms, offering plenty of opportunity for maximizing space. Regardless of their placement, bathroom wall shelves elevate the feeling of your small space, and open shelves serve as a platform for displaying bathroom decor items like bathroom candles, diffusers for the bathroom, and plants.

Boxes are another solution to organizing small items in the bathroom and avoiding clutter. As a bathroom shelf organization idea, they offer generous vessels for makeup or skincare products. Aside from shelving, boxes can be used for bathroom cabinet organization or function as great bathroom drawer organization ideas. Not only do boxes help you stay organized, they bring a personal definition to the space.

6. Consider the right bathroom materials


Consider the bathroom materials that will complement the overall feeling of your bathroom when designing your space. Wood, for example, can create a warm and inviting environment, whereas aluminum and stainless steel can add industrial aesthetic. Storage solutions and shelves are timeless essentials that introduce simplicity to the space. You can also choose wood bathroom shelves for a more natural feel to the bathroom. Chairs for the bathroom can also be considered from a material lens — introducing stainless steel or wood to the space — and can act as additional surface area in addition to seating. What chair can you put in a bathroom? Any chair that is compact in scale will offer a multipurpose addition to this intimate space.
