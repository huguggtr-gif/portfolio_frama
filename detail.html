About the Chair

Petit Rond Chair in stainless steel — a timeless silhouette refined through material mastery. The round seat and curved backrest create a visual softness that contrasts beautifully with the industrial precision of polished steel.

Designed for both indoor and outdoor use, the chair's surface develops a subtle patina over time, becoming uniquely yours.

Materials & Finish

Brushed stainless steel frame with hand-polished seat. Each chair is individually finished, ensuring slight variation that reflects the handmade nature of the process.

Dimensions

Width 46 cm
Depth 50 cm
Height 81 cm
Seat Height 46 cm
Weight 4.2 kg

Care Instructions

Wipe clean with a soft, dry cloth. For outdoor use, store indoors during extended periods of wet weather. Avoid abrasive cleaners that may scratch the polished surface.
